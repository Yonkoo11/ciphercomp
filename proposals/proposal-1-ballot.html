The Problem

In January 2025, the DOJ and FTC warned that sharing compensation data through third parties may violate antitrust law. Glassdoor, Pave, and every salary survey processes your data in plaintext. Someone at those companies can see what you earn.

How This Works

Your salary is encrypted using fully homomorphic encryption (FHE) before it leaves your browser. The smart contract on Fhenix computes on ciphertext. It counts how many salaries fall above each threshold without ever decrypting an individual number. Only the aggregate count is revealed.
