Why encrypted benchmarking

In January 2025, the DOJ and FTC warned that sharing compensation data through third parties may violate antitrust law. The old safe harbor was withdrawn.

CipherComp uses FHE on Fhenix. Your salary is encrypted in your browser. The smart contract counts how many salaries exceed each threshold without decrypting anyone's number. Only the aggregate count is ever revealed.
